🎯 How to Use (3 Simple Steps)

1 Open the Application

Navigate to http://localhost:3000 in your web browser

2 Upload Your Resume

Click "Choose File" and select your resume (PDF, DOC, DOCX, or TXT format)

3 Get Instant Feedback

Click "Analyze Resume" and receive your ATS score, detailed feedback, and improvement suggestions

💡 Key Features

1. Comprehensive Analysis

  • ATS Score: Get a score from 0-100 based on industry standards
  • Section Detection: Identifies Contact, Summary, Experience, Education, Skills, Projects, Certifications
  • Keyword Analysis: Counts action verbs and industry keywords
  • Achievement Tracking: Finds quantifiable metrics and results

2. AI-Powered Feedback

  • Overall Assessment: High-level evaluation of your resume
  • Strengths: What you're doing well
  • Issues: What needs improvement
  • Recommendations: Specific, actionable suggestions
  • Priority Levels: High, Medium, Low impact items

3. Resume Improvement

  • One-Click Generation: Automatically create an improved version
  • Professional Formatting: ATS-optimized structure
  • Enhanced Content: Better action verbs and quantifiable achievements
  • Download Option: Save your improved resume

4. History & Tracking

  • Save All Analyses: Every resume is stored in the database
  • Review Past Results: Click on any previous analysis
  • Track Progress: See how your resume improves over time

🛠️ Technical Stack

Backend

  • Node.js + Express: Server framework
  • SQLite: Database for storing resumes
  • Multer: File upload handling
  • pdf-parse: PDF text extraction
  • mammoth: DOCX text extraction

Frontend

  • HTML5: Semantic markup
  • CSS3: Modern gradients, animations, glassmorphism
  • JavaScript: Interactive functionality
  • Responsive Design: Works on all devices

📊 Understanding Your Score

81-100 (Excellent)

Your resume is ATS-optimized and ready for applications. Minor tweaks can make it even better.

61-80 (Good)

Solid foundation with room for improvement. Focus on the high-priority recommendations.

41-60 (Fair)

Needs significant improvements. Add missing sections and quantifiable achievements.

0-40 (Needs Work)

Major restructuring required. Follow all recommendations and use the improved version as a template.
